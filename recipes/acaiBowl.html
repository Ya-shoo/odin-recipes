Acai Bowl :O

Description

Yummy, cold, refreshing acai bowl perfect for hot summer days or whenever you need a sweet treat :p

Topped with diced banana, granola, chopped nectarines, and a spoonful of almond butter, this summer treat is incredibly delicious.

Ingredients

  • Acai mix or frozen blueberries
  • Chopped Banana
  • Granola
  • Diced Nectarine
  • Almond Butter

Steps:

  1. Blend acai mix or berry mix in blender then place into bowl
  2. Lay chopped banana and other ingredients (granola and necterine)
  3. Add almond butter
  4. Enjoy! :D
