Greek Yogurt Bowl :D

Description

Soft, cooling meal for summer time afternoons. This is a greek yogurt bowl topped with bananas, blueberries, raspberries, chocolate granola, and a coconut nectar drizzle.

Ingredients

  • Greek Yogurt
  • Chopped Banana
  • Berries (blueberries and raspberries)
  • Chocolate Granola
  • Coconut Nectar

Steps:

  1. Pour Greek Yogurt into bowl
  2. Place chopped banana
  3. Place toppings (berries and granola)
  4. Drizzle Coconut Nectar
  5. Enjoy!
