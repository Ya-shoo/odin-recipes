Homemade Nachos Recipe :P

Description

Delicious homemade nachos featuring beans, cilantro, jalapenos, diced onions, and salsa. Mmm mmm mmm!

It comes out piping hot from the air fryer with gooey cheese and a delicious smell of roasted jalapenos, onions, and salsa. The hotter the better I say!

Ingredients:

  • Chips (your choice!)
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Beans (black beans work too)
  • Salsa
  • Jalapenos
  • Diced Onions
  • Cilantro

Steps:

  1. Set air fryer to 375 degrees
  2. Place Chips as neatly as possible, covering the whole plate
  3. Spread Shredded Cheese evenly across each chip and the whole plate
  4. Place Beans on each chip or to your liking
  5. Add Salsa onto each chip or to your liking
  6. Add Jalapenos, Diced Onions, and Cilantro across the whole plate!
  7. Pop your plate into the air fryer and wait 4-8 minutes
  8. Enjoy!! ^o^
